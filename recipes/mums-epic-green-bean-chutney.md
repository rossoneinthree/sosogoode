## Ingredients

•	4 lb green beans , 	•	2 lb chopped onions , boil , 	•	3 cups vinegar , 	•	2 tablespoons chilli powder , 	•	2 dessertspoons coriander , 	•	4 cups sugar , 	•	1 dessertspoon salt

## Info

- **Total:** 60

**Chef:** Mum

**Difficulty:** Easy

*Pour boiling water over onions and stand for 15 minutes.

Cook beans for 15 mins & drain.
Drain onions & add to beans.
Put sauce over beans and onions & boil together (about 15 mins).
Bottle.*
