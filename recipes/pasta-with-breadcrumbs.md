## Method

Heat a little oil in a small sautÃ© pan and gently fry the breadcrumbs until they start to turn
light golden brown. Drain and reserve.

Add a slug of olive oil to a medium sautÃ© pan over a medium heat, then add the butter.
When the butter starts to bubble gently and foam (not brown), stir in the chilli, garlic and
anchovy. Cook for 30 seconds, then add the lemon zest and juice. Remove from the heat. Add the
Parmesan and stir and stir. Keep warm.

Cook the pasta in boiling salted water according to the packet instructions. Drain, reserving
some of the pasta cooking water.

Transfer the pasta to the buttery lemon sauce along with a lug of pasta water. Return to the
heat and stir well to coat the sauce, using a spoon or tongs, ensuring that all the pasta is
shiny and emulsified. Serve piled into bowls with a little more salt and black pepper,
then serve immediately sprinkled with the breadcrumbs.
