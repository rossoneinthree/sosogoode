## Ingredients

1½ cups pitted olives, mix of kalamata and green olives , 4 oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes , 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil , 1 tablespoon capers , ½ tablespoon fresh lemon juice , 1 garlic clove , ⅓ cup fresh basil or parsley

*This olive tapenade recipe is a delicious, easy appetizer! Full of salty, briny flavor, it's a great spread for crackers, baguette, and more.*
