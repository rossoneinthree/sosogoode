## Ingredients

2 medium/large courgettes , 50g pine nuts , 2 handfuls spinach , 2 garlic cloves , Juice of half a lemon (~2 tbsp) , 1 heaped tbsp vegan soft cheese , 1/4 cup/3 tbsp vegan parmesan (or sub nutritional yeast) , 1 cup loosely packed basil , 1/4 cup/3 tbsp olive oil , 400g pasta , Salt and pepper, to taste , Extra vegan parmesan for serving (optional)

*Fresh, light and delicious roasted courgette pesto pasta. Jazz up your next pasta dish with seasonal courgettes. It's the perfect summer pasta!*
