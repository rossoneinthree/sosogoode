## Method

Get a jar of sauerkraut / sliced cucumbers from your local Turkish shop.
Remove the pickling liquor.
Add a spoonful of gochujang Korean chili paste and a splash of sesame oil and you
get instant kimchi.
Spoon on top of pork tacos.
