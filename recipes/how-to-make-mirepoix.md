## Ingredients

2 tablespoons olive oil , 6 cups chopped onions , 4 cups chopped celery , 4 cups chopped carrots

**Source:** [https://www.jerseygirlcooks.com/instant-mirepoix/](https://www.jerseygirlcooks.com/instant-mirepoix/)

*Mirepoix is a classic French base for soups, stews, and sauces, made from chopped onions, celery, and carrots cooked gently in olive oil. It's easy to prepare and perfect for freezing to use as needed in a variety of recipes.*
