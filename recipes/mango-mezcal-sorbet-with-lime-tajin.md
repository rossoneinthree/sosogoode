## Method

Make a mango sorbet with frozen mango pieces (the kind they sell to make
smoothies
Blitz them in a powerful food blender / Ninja Bullet thing. Scrape down the sides until
you have a smooth sorbet.
Add lime juice and pinch of salt (& tajin) to the frozen puree and keep blitzing.
Serve it in a bowl with a shot of Mezcal dumped over the top of it at the table.
