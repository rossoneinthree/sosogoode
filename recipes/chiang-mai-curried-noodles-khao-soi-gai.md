## Ingredients

400ml vegetable oil, plus 1 tbsp , 4 tbsp Thai red or yellow curry paste , 2 tsp mild curry powder , 1 tsp red chilli flakes , 400ml can coconut milk , 150ml coconut cream , 200ml vegetable or chicken stock , 2 tbsp fish sauce , juice of 2 limes , 500g skinless boneless chicken thigh , 300g Chinese dried egg noodles (medium or thin) , 1 long red chilli (deseeded if you don't like it too hot), sliced , 3 small shallots, cut into quarters , 150ml rice wine vinegar , small pack coriander, roughly chopped , 100g beansprouts, trimmed , 3 chopped spring onions , chilli oil

**Source:** [https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/chiang-mai-curried-noodles-khao-soi-gai](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/chiang-mai-curried-noodles-khao-soi-gai)

*This creamy, tangy curry from northern Thailand, with coconut milk and juicy chicken thighs, is topped with pickled shallots and crispy noodles.*
