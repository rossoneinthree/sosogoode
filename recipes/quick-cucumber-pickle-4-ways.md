## Ingredients

oil - olive, sesame or neutral , cucumber i like the small persian ones. , grated or crushed garlic , acid, lemon juice, rice vinegar or even white wine , pepper , gochujang or chili , dill

## Method

slice into as this slices as you can manage, or into little cubes or just smash it up anyway you like. salt liberally and leave it for up to 30 mins. then squeeze out any water, give it a rinse. squeeze some more, then add an oil, an acid, the garlic and chili or dill
