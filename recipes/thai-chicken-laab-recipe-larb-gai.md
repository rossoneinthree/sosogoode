## Ingredients

300 g (11 oz) ground chicken, preferably not lean (dark meat preferred) , 2 Tbsp (30 ml) water or unsalted chicken stock (use stock only if you already have it) , 1 Tbsp + 2 teaspoons (25 ml) fish sauce , 1 small shallot, thinly sliced (about 1/4 cup) , 2 Tbsp (30 ml) fresh lime juice , 1-2 teaspoons (5-10 ml) roasted chili flakes, or spicy chili powder to taste , 2-3 Tbsp (30-45 ml) toasted rice powder (see below for recipe) , 6-8 sprigs cilantro , 2-3 leaves sawtooth coriander (optional, or substitute more cilantro) , 1 green onion, chopped , 1/3 cup mint leaves, roughly torn if large , Sticky rice for serving , Fresh veggies for serving (cabbage, long beans, cucumber, romaine lettuce, carrots, etc.) , Crispy chicken skin for garnish (optional) , Toasted Rice Powder: , 3 Tbsp uncooked Thai sticky rice or jasmine rice , 1 makrut (kaffir) lime leaf (optional)

**Source:** [https://hot-thai-kitchen.com/laab-gai/](https://hot-thai-kitchen.com/laab-gai/)

*Laab gai (aka larb gai) is a spicy and tart chicken salad iconic of northeastern Thai cuisine. This refreshing salad boasts a punchy lime and fish sauce dressing, is packed with fresh herbs, and is served with sticky rice and crunchy vegetables.
