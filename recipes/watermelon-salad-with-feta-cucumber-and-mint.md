## Ingredients

2 tablespoons honey , 2 tablespoons lime juice , 1 to 2 tablespoons quality extra virgin olive oil , Pinch of salt , 1/2 watermelon, peeled and cut into cubes , 1 English or Hot House cucumber, cubed (about 2 cupfuls of cubed cucumbers) , 15 fresh mint leaves, chopped , 15 fresh basil leaves, chopped , 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese (more to your liking)

**Source:** [https://www.themediterraneandish.com/watermelon-salad-with-cucumber-feta/](https://www.themediterraneandish.com/watermelon-salad-with-cucumber-feta/)

*Watermelon salad is the essence of summer! This refreshing combination of sweet watermelon, cool cucumbers, salty feta, fresh mint, and an easy honey-lime dressing makes for a perfect sweet-and-salty summer salad.*
