## Ingredients

2.5 lb (1.15kg) breast of lamb , 1-2 onions, sliced , 2-3 carrots, sliced , A bunch of herbs (such as thyme, bay, parsley) , 2-3 oz (about 60-85g) bacon or salt pork (optional) , Salt and pepper, to taste , About 1 pint (570ml) water , Prepared mustard , 1 egg, beaten (enough for 2.5 lb meat) , Breadcrumbs (homemade from a dried French loaf, preferably) , A little melted butter, for grilling

**Chef:** Elizabeth David

**Source:** [https://sheepsheadfood.com/2014/01/18/elizabeth-davids-breast-of-lamb-ste-menehould/](https://sheepsheadfood.com/2014/01/18/elizabeth-davids-breast-of-lamb-ste-menehould/)

*A classic French dish of lamb breast, braised then pressed, sliced, coated in mustard, egg, and breadcrumbs, then baked and grilled until crisp. Traditionally served with a sharp oil-based sauce.*
