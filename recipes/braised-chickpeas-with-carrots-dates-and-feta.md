## Ingredients

- 300g dried chickpeas, soaked overnight in plenty of cold water
- 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
- 1 onion, peeled and roughly chopped
- 6 garlic cloves, roughly chopped
- 2cm piece fresh ginger, peeled and roughly chopped
- 1 large green chilli, roughly chopped, seeds and all
- 15g coriander leaves, roughly chopped
- 75ml olive oil
- 1Â½ tsp ground cumin
- 1Â½ tsp ground cinnamon
- 2 medjool dates, pitted and roughly chopped
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 4 carrots, peeled and each cut at an angle into 2 or 3 large chunks (450g)
- 2 bay leaves
- Â¼ tsp bicarbonate of soda
- Salt and black pepper
- 1-2 lemons, zest finely grated to get 1Â½ tsp, and juiced to get 2 tbsp
- 120g feta, roughly crumbled
- 1 tsp caraway seeds, toasted and roughly crushed
- 1-2 tbsp parsley leaves, roughly chopped

## Method

1. Heat the oven to 180C (160C fan)/350F/gas 4.

2. Drain the soaked chickpeas and set aside.

3. Put the onion, garlic, ginger and chilli in a food processor, and pulse a few times until very finely chopped but not pureed, scraping down the sides of the bowl as you go. Add the coriander, and pulse a couple of times more, just to mix through.

4. On a medium-high flame, heat two tablespoons of oil in a large, heavy-based cast-iron pot with a lid. Add the onion mixture and cook for about four minutes, stirring occasionally, then stir in the cumin, cinnamon, dates and tomato paste, and cook for a minute more, or until fragrant.

5. Add the drained chickpeas, carrots, bay leaves, bicarbonate of soda, 1.2 litres water and a good grind of black pepper, and bring to a boil, skimming off any froth that comes to the surface. Cover with the lid, put in the oven and bake for two hours, or until the chickpeas are very soft and the sauce has turned thick and rich.

6. Stir in the lemon juice and two teaspoons of salt, then leave to cool for about 10 minutes.

7. While the chickpeas are cooking, put the feta in a small bowl with the caraway, lemon zest, parsley and remaining three tablespoons of olive oil, and leave to marinate.

8. To serve, spoon the feta mixture over the chickpeas and serve directly from the cooking pot.

- **Serves:** 4-6
