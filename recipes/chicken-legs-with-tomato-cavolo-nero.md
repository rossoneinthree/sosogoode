## Ingredients

4 chicken legs , 400g cherry tomatoes , 2 tablespoons capers , 100g olives (pitted) ,  1 red chilli (sliced) , 200g cavolo nero (chopped) , 2 tablespoons olive oil , salt and pepper to taste

## Method

1 - Preheat your oven to 200Â°C (400Â°F).


2 - In a large ovenproof pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.


3 -  Season the chicken legs with salt and pepper, then place them in the pan, skin side down.


4 - Sear the chicken legs for about 5-7 minutes until the skin is golden brown.


5 -  Flip the chicken legs over and add the cherry tomatoes, capers, olives, and sliced chilli to the pan.


6 - Stir gently to combine the ingredients.


7 - Transfer the pan to the preheated oven and roast for 25-30 minutes.


8 - After 25 minutes, add the chopped cavolo nero to the pan, stirring it in with the other ingredients.


9 - Return the pan to the oven and cook for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cavolo nero is tender.


10 - Remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
