## Ingredients

1/2 onion , 4 cloves garlic, peeled , 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary leaves, or 1 tablespoon dried , Zest of 1 lemon , 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar , 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil , Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper , 1 boneless leg of lamb, 5 to 6 pounds, butterflied

**Source:** [https://www.simplyrecipes.com/recipes/grilled_butterflied_leg_of_lamb/](https://www.simplyrecipes.com/recipes/grilled_butterflied_leg_of_lamb/)

*This leg of lamb is butterflied for easy grilling and marinated with rosemary and garlic to give it great flavor. Make it for a big summer cook-out, or for a twist on Easter dinner.*
